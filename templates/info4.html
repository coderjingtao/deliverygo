Inner Melbourne community legal

Inner Melbourne Community Legal is a not-for profit community organisation that provides free legal assistance to disadvantaged people in the City of Melbourne area. It is a member of the Federation of Community Legal Centres and the National Association of Community Legal Centres.

Federation of Community Legal Centres (Vic)

The Federation of Community Legal Centres (Victoria) is the peak body for 49 community legal centres (CLCs) across Victoria. As the peak body for community legal centres, they lead and support excellence in the community legal sector, promote the purpose and value of community legal centres, and advance social justice and a fair legal system. Community legal centres are independent, not for profit community organisations that provide free legal and related services to the public, with a focus on disadvantaged, vulnerable and marginalised community members.

Location: Level 3/225 Bourke St, Melbourne VIC 3000

Opening Hours: 9am - 5pm (Mon to Fri)
