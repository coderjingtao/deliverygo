Home > Road Rules > Riding at night

Rider Guideline: All Legal Information are retrieved from VicRoads

 

If you are riding a bicycle at night you must have:

1.White light (flashing or steady) on the front

2.Red light (flashing or steady) on the back

3.A red reflector on the back

Note:

Both front light and rear red light should be visible from at least 200 metres and the red rear reflector visible from at least 50 metres
